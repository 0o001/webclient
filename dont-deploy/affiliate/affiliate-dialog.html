For new users:
New users who register through your affiliate URLs are your referrals. When your referred users subsequently purchase within 12 months, you will qualify for a commission.
Any MEGA user can participate
Existing MEGA users who purchase within 24 hours after having most recently visited any of your affiliate URLs will qualify you for a commission.
Depending on your country, we offer a variety of redemption methods, including bank transfers & Bitcoin.
You must have earned at least €50 to qualify for a redemption.
You must make at least one real payment to MEGA to qualify for a redemption.
Depending on your country and redemption method, we may charge you a redemption fee.
You must agree and comply with the Terms of Service.
For file or folder links to qualify you must own worldwide commercial usage rights to the data you share. If your file or folder links contain content created by someone else, you must have their written permission to use it.
A qualifying commission will have a 90 day grace period before it is redeemable.
A legally valid takedown notice will reverse the commissions earned against the relevant file or folder URL(s).
A copyright strike will disqualify your account from participation for 90 days.
Repeated strikes will permanently disqualify you from participation.
